# Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)

**ChangeGuard AI — Predictive Risk Scoring for Change Advisory Board Decisioning**

> Structured around the project's seven delivery stages (18 weeks discovery-to-launch) plus ongoing post-launch governance. See the [README](./README.md) for full project context and the [Scope Statement](./03-Scope-Statement.md) for in/out-of-scope boundaries that bound this WBS.

---

## 1.0 ChangeGuard AI Project

### 1.1 Discovery & Business Case
- 1.1.1 Stakeholder discovery interviews (CAB Chair, Head of IT Risk & Change Management, SRE leads, Model Risk Validation)
- 1.1.2 Historical change/incident data pull from ServiceNow (3 years)
- 1.1.3 Baseline quantification — failure rate, cost per failed change, CAB dwell time
- 1.1.4 Scope-setting workshop (auto-approve → advisory scoring reframe)
- 1.1.5 Project Charter drafted
- 1.1.6 Business Case drafted
- 1.1.7 Sponsor approval gate
- 1.1.8 Model Risk Validation pre-approval gate

### 1.2 Requirements & Data Readiness Assessment
- 1.2.1 Data audit of ~21,000 historical change records
- 1.2.2 Change-to-incident linkage reliability assessment
- 1.2.3 Cross-functional failure-taxonomy workshop and sign-off
- 1.2.4 SME relabeling of ambiguous historical records (4-week effort)
- 1.2.5 Functional & non-functional requirements definition
- 1.2.6 Explainability requirement specification (SHAP-based, per-prediction)
- 1.2.7 Product Requirements Document (PRD) finalized
- 1.2.8 Data Readiness Assessment finalized

### 1.3 Solution Design & Architecture
- 1.3.1 Model architecture options analysis (deep learning ensemble vs. gradient-boosted trees vs. third-party SaaS)
- 1.3.2 Build-versus-buy evaluation (ServiceNow native vs. third-party vendor)
- 1.3.3 Model Risk Validation consultation on architecture options
- 1.3.4 Human-in-the-loop and score-display design (top-3 contributing factors shown by default)
- 1.3.5 Divergence-rationale logging design
- 1.3.6 Architecture Decision Record (ADR) finalized

### 1.4 Build & Delivery Coordination *(9 two-week sprints)*
- 1.4.1 Sprints 1–3: Data foundation — cleaned dataset, SME-relabeled sample, feature pipeline v1
- 1.4.2 Sprints 4–5: Model development — XGBoost baseline model, initial SHAP integration
- 1.4.3 Sprint 6: Feature enrichment — timing-related risk features (weekend/holiday submission pattern)
- 1.4.4 Sprints 7–8: Validation prep & expanded bias testing (scope added via change request)
- 1.4.5 Sprint 9: UAT & launch readiness — CAB UAT, go-live runbook drafted
- 1.4.6 Weekly RAID log reviews with sponsor (ongoing across all sprints)
- 1.4.7 Change request management (expanded bias testing scope addition)

### 1.5 Evaluation, Testing & Model Risk Review
- 1.5.1 Model evaluation against held-out historical test set (recall, precision, calibration)
- 1.5.2 Expanded bias/fairness testing across business units
- 1.5.3 Root-cause investigation of flagging disparity (legacy-systems team)
- 1.5.4 Feature remediation — system age and technical-debt indicators added
- 1.5.5 CAB User Acceptance Testing (UAT) — explanation usefulness survey
- 1.5.6 Independent Model Validation process and documentation package
- 1.5.7 Documentation remediation (Stage 2 relabeling methodology gap)
- 1.5.8 Independent Model Validation sign-off

### 1.6 Deployment & Change Management
- 1.6.1 Go-live runbook with rollback triggers and decision ownership
- 1.6.2 CAB training — focused on reading explanations, not just the label
- 1.6.3 Phase 1 — Pilot (core banking domain, ~150 changes/month, 3 weeks)
- 1.6.4 Phase 2 — Expanded pilot (payments & digital channel domains, ~250 changes/month, 3 weeks)
- 1.6.5 Phase 3 — Full rollout (remaining in-scope domains, ~200 changes/month, phased over 4 weeks)
- 1.6.6 Daily override-pattern monitoring (week 1 of pilot)
- 1.6.7 Communications plan execution (CAB members, affected tech teams, leadership)
- 1.6.8 Sponsor written commitment re: non-use of score in performance evaluation

### 1.7 Post-Launch Governance & Continuous Improvement *(ongoing)*
- 1.7.1 Quarterly disparity monitoring across business units
- 1.7.2 Weekly override-rationale compliance tracking
- 1.7.3 Monthly change-failure-rate tracking against business case targets
- 1.7.4 Annual full model revalidation (or on significant drift)
- 1.7.5 Metric-gaming / behavioral adaptation investigation (quarterly review)
- 1.7.6 Cost-per-score monitoring against Stage 1 forecast
- 1.7.7 Lessons-learned capture
- 1.7.8 Forward roadmap definition (domain expansion, retraining feedback loop, fast-track evaluation)
